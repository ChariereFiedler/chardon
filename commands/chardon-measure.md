---
description: Measure the ROI of an applied Chardon action (friction before vs after).
---

Re-measure the friction an applied action targeted and record its ROI delta.

1. Run: `node ${CLAUDE_PLUGIN_ROOT}/dist/roi-actions.mjs measure <ACTION_ID>`
2. Report the before → after friction counts and the reduction. The result appears in
   the "Measured ROI" section of `/chardon-improve`.
